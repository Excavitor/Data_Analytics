Yearly Total Sales in Each Division

Financial Analytics / Store and Time / Analytics 1

This chart shows the Yearly total sales in each division from 2014 to 2022. Here are some of the key findings from the chart:
  1. Total sales have quietly increased in a few divisions over the past eight years. The most significant sales were in Dhaka, where sales from 2014 to 2016 significantly increased, but in 2017, a sudden drop but at least the starting year. Then, there was an increase in growth till 2020; after that, sales dropped each year. Rajshahi, Khulna, Sylhet, and Mymensing also show up and down sales. Meanwhile, sales in the other divisions grew less significantly.
  2. Dhaka is the clear leader in terms of total sales. 2016 Dhaka's sales were 698,523(tk), the highest in all divisions. Rajshahi, Khulna, Sylhet, and Mymensing were the following closest divisions.
